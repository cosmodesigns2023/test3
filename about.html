About Us

Introduction

MANUFACTURER & EXPORTER OF SESAME SEED OIL

We are one of India's most recognised and dependable manufacturers and exporters of sesame oil, with decades of expertise in the food industry. We have been in sesame oil manufacturing business since 1957 and have carved out a niche for ourselves among both domestic and international buyers.

  • Leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality sesame oil.
  • Premium-grade sesame oil for culinary and cosmetic use.
  • Trusted global supplier for pure, natural sesame oil.

SINCE 1957

OUR HISTORY

Shagun Enterprises was established 1957, when Mr. Kimatram Phoolwani began manufacturing sesame seeds, and the rest, as they say, is history. Mr. Kimatram Phoolwani has been offering consumers with excellent quality, natural, hulled, sortex and sun-dried sesame seeds since 1957. He began the packaging of the brand 'Sant,' which is still sold under Shagun Enterprises. The business has been passed down from generation to generation, and it is presently managed by Mr Kimatram's great grandson Mr. Dinesh Kumar Phoolwani.
